Awards of Imagine Dragons


In 2014 Imagine dragons won their first Grammy's award. This award was
for having the best rock performance at the Grammy's ceremony. Their performance
featured their hit song "radioactive", which featured rapper Kendrick Lamar. Many
audience members wondered how they would mix both artist and band to create a performance.
However, once they started performing, slowly people started to get up from there seat and
feel the rythm music as almost the whole audience was up. Once began to rap
his part, their was a red glowy spray paint amongst imagine dragons and Dan Reynolds (the lead singer)

Facts About Lead Singer Dan Reynolds

  • Imagine Dragons hit it big with their 2012 album Night Visions which features the single "Radioactive."
  • He formed Imagine Dragons while studying at Brigham Young University. He also worked at a Mormon missionary for two years.
  • He and his wife Aja Volkman released an eponymous EP under the name Egyptian.
  • He was the seventh of nine children raised by Ronald and Christene Reynolds.
