Credit Approval

- Credit Approval - 

Credit approval contains a credit.csv file that will read the file and store them into a list.
The file contains over 100 rows of data and 10 columns of data. Data is converted, such that the '?'
is replaced with '0'. The word, male is converted to '1' and female is converted to '0'.

Data is then split and shows the accuracy of the data in the list using sklearn and metrics. 
The training test was tested multiple times and shows that it is above 80% majority of the time.
